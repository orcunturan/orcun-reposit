Call for Participants

Study title: Productive and Consumable: The Construction of Stigma as an Asset

Overview

I’m recruiting participants for a qualitative study on consumers’ experiences with psychosocial and pharmacological treatments for ADHD in healthcare settings in Canada. The project examines how discourses, meanings, and interpretations of disability shift through market actors and practices.

Who can participate?

  • Adults living with ADHD
  • Currently residing in Canada
  • Have been in treatment for ADHD using private solutions, including online services

What participation involves

A confidential, in-depth interview (remote or in person, depending on location and preference). We’ll discuss your experiences with access, treatment, and everyday management.

Details such as interview length, scheduling, and consent information will be provided when you email to express interest.

Email orcunt@yorku.ca to participate

Research Ethics Approval

This study has been reviewed and approved by the York University Office of Research Ethics. Certificate Number: STU 2024-086.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Participation is voluntary. Your information will be handled confidentially and any reporting will use pseudonyms and de-identification practices. You may skip questions or withdraw at any time.

Questions? Email orcunt@yorku.ca.
